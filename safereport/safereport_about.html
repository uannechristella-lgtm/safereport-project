About SafeReport

"Our mission is to break the silence by giving every individual a private, dignified way to seek help. We believe that physical distance or fear of judgement should never be a barrier to justice. By combining secure technology with community action, we empower the vulnerable to take control of their situation and ensure that no one is left to suffer in the shadows."

our vision

"We are dedicated to strengthening the community support ecosystem. By providing a 3-minute reporting tool, we remove the emotional and physical barriers to seeking aid. Our goal is to transform real-time community data into faster responses from NGOs and leaders, ensuring resources reach those who need them most while protecting the human dignity of all users."

How It Works

  1. Open the app on any phone
    SafeReport works on any phone or computer with a browser. No installation needed. Simply open the link from home, in private, at any time of day.
  2. Fill in the Report Form
    Choose your problem category such as Health, Legal, Financial, Shelter, or Other. Describe your situation in simple words. You can choose to stay completely anonymous if you wish.
  3. Receive Your Reference Code
    After submitting, you receive a unique reference code. A trusted support team views your report and connects you to the right organization that can help you.

Who This App Helps

  • Refugees and Displaced Persons: People who do not know the local system or language and need guidance on available support.
  • Elderly People: Older individuals who struggle to navigate complex service systems or travel long distances.
  • People with Disabilities: Those who face physical or social barriers that make in-person visits difficult.
  • Low-Income Families: Families who cannot afford to lose a day of work waiting in offices.
  • Youth Without Support: Young people without family guidance who need direction and access to social services.
  • Abuse Survivors: Individuals in sensitive situations who need help but fear exposure.
